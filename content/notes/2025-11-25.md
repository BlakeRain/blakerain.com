---
title: Supermaven is shutting down
link: https://supermaven.com/blog/sunsetting-supermaven
date: 2025-11-25
---

Supermaven have decided that they're going to [shut down] after their acquisition by Cursor.
According to their blog post, they're still going to be offering their service for free to their
NeoVim and JetBrains customers:

> While deciding the future of Supermaven, we heard feedback from our existing Neovim and JetBrains
> customers about their love for Supermaven. We will continue to provide autocomplete inference free
> for these existing customers.

In October [GitHub] decided that I no longer qualify to receive Copilot Pro [for free] as a
maintainer of FOSS projects. I'm not entirely surprised, as I've not contributed to FOSS projects in
a while.

I started using Supermaven as I still find it handy to have my [`AsRef`] traits generated, and I can
never remember how to handle [time] in C++. Supermaven's NeoVim plugin works quite nicely too, so
I'd prefer to keep being able to use it.
