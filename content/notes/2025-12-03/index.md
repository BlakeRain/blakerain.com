My poor OKI ML3390 suffered a collapse of one of the tractor frame assemblies: the sprocketed track
had come apart with age. Finding new parts for old dot matrix printers is getting harder. The
second-hand market is turning into a a good place to look for parts. I found a seller called
[theprinterbroker] on eBay, and they had exactly what I needed.

The new tractor part for the OKI 3390 arrived today. As it's been several centuries since I've had
to do anything with printers, I found a [YouTube] video that shows a breakdown of replacing the
component.
